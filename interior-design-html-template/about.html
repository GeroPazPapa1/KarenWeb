Mom, Lighting Designer, Entrepreneur.

In 1992, my husband Alejandro and I took a leap of faith, founding TRAZZO Lighting together. It's been a labor of love ever since. Fast forward to 2020, and with a heart full of passion and determination, we made the bold decision to expand our dream to Miami, spreading our light to new places.

So, that's a little about me – a mom, a lighting designer, and an entrepreneur. And with every project, I pour my heart into not just illuminating spaces, but brightening lives with warmth and joy

Believing in the magic of light

As Frank Lloyd Wright once said, “Light is the beautifier of the building” It's this belief in the transformative power of light that drives me, understanding that good light isn't just an option – it's a necessity. From Louis Kahn's notion that 'Architecture is the thoughtful making of spaces,' to Zaha Hadid's vision that 'Architecture is really about well-being,' I've learned that lighting plays a pivotal role in shaping how we perceive and experience these spaces.

5000 Projects | 30 Years of experience | 5 Countries

My love for travel, fashion, design, and Miami

Traveling has always been my thing. There's just something magical about exploring new cities, meeting new people, and collecting stories along the way. It's my favorite way to unwind and recharge. Fashion is my way of expressing myself. Whether I'm scouting unique pieces abroad or browsing the latest trends in Miami, fashion always adds a little extra sparkle to my day. Miami is like a second home to me. There's just something about Miami's vibe that makes me feel in my place– the energy, the people, the whole shebang.
